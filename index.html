About Me

I am Aybüke Küçük, a Geomatics Engineering student at Hacettepe University with a strong interest in mapping technologies, spatial data analysis, and modern geospatial tools. During my studies, I have built a solid background in cartography, GNSS positioning, and remote sensing. I am particularly passionate about photogrammetry and data science, as I enjoy exploring how spatial data can be processed, analyzed, and visualized to solve real-world problems.
